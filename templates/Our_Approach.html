Our Approach

Understand Yourself Better

At "Mental Health Awareness Test," we guide individuals towards emotional well-being and healthy social relations.
Our approach involves a series of tailored assessments covering anger, academic stress, anxiety, and depression.
Participants journey through thought-provoking questions, adapted based on previous responses, to uncover deeper insights.
We encourage regular participation to unveil hidden issues and empower informed decisions.
Beyond scores, we prioritize self-care, fostering a culture of compassion and understanding.
Together, let's embark on this transformative journey towards inner peace.
